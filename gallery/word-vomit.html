Word Vomit

This was originally designed for my pen plotter. I wanted to have a bunch of words spread across the screen and I liked the idea of being able to spew them from my mouse like a sparkler.

The lyrics for Mr. Roger's famous song Won't You Be My Neighbor were initially placeholder text -- I had been thinking of him a lot since re-watching his documentary (MUST WATCH) -- But I loved them so much I decided to keep them permanently :)
